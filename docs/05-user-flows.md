# User Flows

## Landing Page

Focused product explanation with primary action to start a workspace and secondary action to view the demo. First viewport should show the product name, short value proposition, and credible UI preview.

## Sign Up

Collect name, email, password, and terms acceptance. Create user, send verification email, and start organization onboarding.

## Login

Email/password, forgot-password link, remembered active sessions, and clear error states.

## Organization Onboarding

Create organization, choose industry, invite teammates, pick role defaults, and land in the dashboard.

## Main Dashboard

Show active projects, overdue items, recent decisions, documents, activity, risks, unanswered questions, and suggested priorities.

## AI Business Assistant

Question input, scoped filters, answer with sources, confidence, limitations, related projects, and follow-up prompts.

## Projects

List, filter, create, archive, and inspect projects. Details include timeline, objectives, decisions, documents, members, and activity.

## Knowledge Explorer

Search, filter, inspect, tag, and connect knowledge items across projects, customers, and sources.

## Documents

Upload, process, extract text, summarize, connect to projects, search content, and manage permissions.

## Integrations

Mock catalog with provider status, expected permissions, sync state, and future roadmap.

## Team And Permissions

Invite members, change roles, remove access, and review permission-sensitive activity.

## Activity Timeline

Chronological audit of important events with filters by project, user, source, and event type.

## Settings

Organization profile, security settings, AI configuration, data retention, and audit export.
