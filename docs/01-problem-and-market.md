# Problem And Market

Knowledge-based companies lose context across documents, conversations, tools, and people. Owners and managers often cannot quickly answer basic operational questions: why a project is delayed, what decision was made, where the latest document is, or what risks need attention.

## Target Market

- Small and medium-sized businesses.
- Software companies.
- Digital agencies.
- Consulting firms.
- Accounting firms.
- Other knowledge-heavy service companies.

## Differentiation

CRM systems track sales relationships; Novera tracks company operating knowledge across customers, projects, decisions, risks, and documents.

Project-management tools track tasks; Novera explains context across tasks, documents, decisions, and history.

Knowledge bases store articles; Novera connects knowledge to active business operations.

BI dashboards show metrics; Novera answers contextual questions with evidence.

Ordinary AI chatbots generate responses; Novera retrieves authorized company sources and states limits when evidence is missing.

## Validation Assumptions

- Teams will manually add high-value context before integrations exist.
- Source-cited answers are more trusted than generic AI summaries.
- Project and decision memory is a strong wedge into SMB adoption.
- Buyers care about risk visibility and weekly management priorities.
