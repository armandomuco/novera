# Target Users

## Business Owner

Needs a clear view of projects, risks, customer context, priorities, and decisions without asking multiple people.

## Manager

Needs to understand project status, blockers, overdue items, ownership, and recent changes.

## Employee

Needs fast access to the latest project information, customer history, internal decisions, and documents.

## Operations Or Admin User

Needs to manage members, permissions, organization settings, integrations, and auditability.

## Viewer Or External Collaborator

Needs read-only access to selected knowledge without exposing broader company information.
