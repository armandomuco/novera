# Product Overview

Novera is an AI Digital Twin for Businesses. It connects company knowledge from documents, projects, decisions, customers, meetings, tasks, and future integrations, then turns that information into a searchable, permission-aware operating memory.

The product is not an ordinary chatbot. It is a structured business context system that answers with sources, reveals gaps, and helps teams understand what happened, why it happened, what changed, and what deserves attention.

## Smallest Realistic MVP

- Authentication and organizations.
- Roles: Owner, Administrator, Manager, Member, Viewer.
- Projects with members, status, objectives, decisions, documents, and activity.
- Manual knowledge items.
- Document upload for PDF, DOCX, TXT, and Markdown.
- Document text extraction architecture.
- Hybrid search architecture.
- AI assistant interface that only answers from authorized sources.
- Dashboard using sample data until analytics mature.
- Mock integration catalog.

## Not In Version One

- Full email/calendar/repository ingestion.
- Autonomous task execution.
- Deep BI charting.
- Enterprise SSO.
- Custom workflow automation.
- Mobile application.
- Fine-grained graph visualization.
